Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025

Welcome to ProBadminton ("we", "our", or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use ProBadminton:

  • Account information: Your name, email address, and profile photo when you sign in with Google.
  • Profile data: Your username, country, and city that you choose to provide.
  • Match data: Match scores, results, and statistics that you log in the app.
  • Usage data: Basic app usage information to improve the app.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve the ProBadminton app features.
  • To show your stats, match history, and leaderboard rankings.
  • To enable social features like friends, groups, and rivalries.
  • To calculate and display tournament results.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase (Firestore and Firebase Authentication), which is hosted on Google's servers. We do not sell your data to any third parties.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Firebase – Authentication and database storage.
  • Google Sign-In – For account login.
  • Google AdMob – To show advertisements in the free version of the app.

5. Children's Privacy

ProBadminton is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

6. Account Deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time directly from within the app. Go to Profile → Delete Account. This will permanently remove all your data from our servers.

7. Your Rights (Indian Users)

As a user in India, you have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To make a request, contact us at the email below.

8. Changes to This Policy
